Lets validate our email address

It should contain a @ and a . after the @
There should be at least two characters after the .

*** Note: this is v basic validation. Take a look for an example using a regex to validate email addresses ***

joe 			invalid
joe@blogg		invalid
joe@blogg.com		valid
joe.bloggs@gmail.com	valid 
joe.bloggs@gmail.	invalid
joe.bloggs@.gmail	invalid
